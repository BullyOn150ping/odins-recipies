Beef Soup Recipe

Description

Easy and delicious soup with a nice thickness. You can make it in the Instant PotÂ® or on the stovetop. When this cools, it will be very thick, but it thins out when reheated. Freezes very well.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans of beef consomme
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
  • 4 carrots, finely chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons pearl barley
  • half teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
